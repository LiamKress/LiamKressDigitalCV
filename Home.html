The Public Broadcasting Domain (Founded by Liam Kress)

Station PBDTV.99

The Public Broadcasting Domain has been the industry leader in localized LP Broadcasting since 1968, focusing on non-commercial, free-to-air television with its headquarters in Burlington, North Carolina. We are publicly funded and specialize in funneling airtime to clients in need of pushing their creative work to our audience of over 300 member television stations, each with thousands of active viewers each day.

Operations & Monetizations

While the Public Broadcasting Domain is a non-profit organization with focuses in education & program distribution, we actively work with adjacent television content providors and related services to cooperatively own our network. In a commercial broadcast television network structure, affiliatess give up portions of their local advertising airtime in ecxhange for network programming, and the PBD pays the affiliates a share of the revenue earned from advertising.
